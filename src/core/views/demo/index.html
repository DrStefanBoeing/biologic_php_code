bioLOGIC Examples

The bioLOGIC project provides project-tailored, interactive visualizations of bioinformatics results, project relevant context and a password-protected platform to share your results privately with co-workers. In addition it endeavours to organise all project relevant information, such as PowerPoint presentations, QC results and actual data results in one central space. As this is a Crick project, your data is kept private at all times. Once your results are ready for publication, you may use the bioLOGIC data display to leverage the reach of your article by having an article-accompanying data website.

More demo projects will be added in this space in the next few days.

Below you find links to some example literature data analysis projects. If you think the bioLOGIC project could help in your research, please come and speak to Stefan Boeing in Bioinformatics ( stefan.boeing@crick.ac.uk ). Rendering your data on the bioLOGIC plattform will take about half a day.
