Diagnosis

  • Biological Datasets have become big, but not yet big and comprehensive enough for AI/Machine learning
  • A better utilization of existing lab- and world knowledge/data is required
  • Scattered Excel Sheets and Static Plots are NOT the way forward
  • It would be desirable to make it easy to discuss results and hypotheses whenever and whereever
  • Maintain your data and results in an accessible state
  • Results and quality measures should be linked
  • Enable (not enforce!) data and result sharing
